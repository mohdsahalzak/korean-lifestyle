Traditional Korean Lifestyle

Hanbok (Traditional Clothing)

Hanbok is the traditional attire of Korea, characterized by vibrant colors and simple lines. It is worn during major festivals and holidays like Seollal (Lunar New Year) and Chuseok (Korean Thanksgiving).

Chuseok (Korean Thanksgiving)

Chuseok is a major harvest festival celebrated by Koreans, similar to Thanksgiving. Families gather to share food, honor ancestors, and perform traditional rituals.

Korean Tea Ceremony

The Korean Tea Ceremony, or Darye, is a peaceful and meditative ritual that highlights respect, grace, and the art of serving tea. It is often accompanied by quiet reflection.
